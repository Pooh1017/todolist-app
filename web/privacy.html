Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-14

This Privacy Policy explains how the To-Do List application ("we", "our", "app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication): When you sign in with Google, we may receive your email address and basic profile information such as display name.
  • To-do content you create: Task and category data you create in the app (for example: title, date, note, done, starred, category name).
  • Technical/API data (limited): When you use our API (via Firebase Cloud Functions), requests may include authentication tokens and standard request metadata necessary for security and troubleshooting.
  • Local storage (on-device): The app may store certain settings and login-related data locally (e.g., last login time and current user email) to improve app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the app.
  • To create, store, and manage your tasks and categories.
  • To sync and retrieve your data from our backend services.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Firebase Firestore: Your tasks and categories may be stored under paths like users/<uid>/tasks and users/<uid>/categories.
  • On your device: Some app settings may be stored locally on your device.

Deletion of Tasks

When you delete a task, it may be marked as deleted (soft delete) in the database rather than immediately removed, to support app behavior and data integrity.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase services to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In)
  • Firebase Firestore (database)
  • Firebase Cloud Functions (API backend)

These services may process data according to Google's policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With Firebase/Google as required to provide the app services.

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data, including authentication and access controls. However, no system is completely secure.

Data Retention
